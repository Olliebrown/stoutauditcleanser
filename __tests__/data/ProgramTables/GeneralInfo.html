In-Progress Repeat Coursework

RQ-10196: If you are repeating coursework beyond the allowable course limit, the in-progress course will be included in your Academic Advisement Report. Please be aware that the highest grade will be used when computing your grade point average. All grades remain on the transcript.
REMEDIAL/PLACEMENT COURSEWORK INFORMATION

If you have taken any remedial and/or placement courses, they will appear here.
Incomplete/In-Progress Coursework Notice

Not Satisfied:   RQ-0169: Incomplete/In Progress/Courses in Registration Status

Incomplete Coursework, that has earned a Grade of an "I", must be completed within one year from the date the "I" is received or an "F" Grade will be assigned.

A grade of "IP" is given for approved courses in which more than one term is required for completion because of the nature of the course requirement. If the course requirements are not completed within two years from the date the "IP" is received, the student must re-register for the course to obtain credit.

Please take the following courses into consideration when planning your future class registration.

Graduation Information

RQ-10197: Registration and Records will verify and certify your completion of your degree requirements. It is your responsibility to work with your Academic Advisor to ensure your successful completion of all program and University requirements.

You cannot graduate with Incomplete, Not Reported, or Progress notations remaining on your record. Transcripts and diplomas cannot be released until Hold notations are cleared.

AN "APPLICATION FOR DEGREE" THROUGH SELF SERVICE MUST BE SUBMITTED NO LATER THAN THE FIRST TWO WEEKS OF THE TERM IN WHICH YOU WILL BE COMPLETING YOUR FINAL DEGREE REQUIREMENTS TO ENSURE THAT YOU HAVE THE OPPORTUNITY TO RESOLVE ANY DEFICIENCIES.

You will be notified by email and/or letter informing you of any deficiencies that you need to meet. Failure to correct these deficiencies will result in denial of your Application for Graduation. If you do not graduate when anticipated, you must re-apply to graduate in the next semester.

Please visit the Commencement Homepage for further information regarding commencement. Direct any questions regarding Diplomas and/or graduation requirements to Registration and Records.
Graduation With Honors Policy

RQ-10209: UNIVERSITY GPA REQUIREMENT FOR GRADUATION

Transfer grades do NOT count in your cumulative Institutional GPA as seen in your UW-Stout record, but they ARE figured into your GPA to determine graduation honors. A minimum cumulative grade point average of 3.50 must have been earned from ALL institutions attended to graduate with honors.

For commencement purposes, the cumulative grade point average earned at the end of the semester preceding the commencement ceremony will determine recognition at the commencement ceremony. However, honors will be determined officially when all coursework has been completed. Honors will be listed on your transcript and diploma.

Please see the Registration and Records website for complete details.
